Hey there! This is a digital polaroid.

People in 21st century, espacially young people, really like recording their life in this way.

In this polaroid, you can click "take a photo" capture what is currently in the camera.

After taking the photo, you can click the sticker you like

and then put the stickers on the picture by clicking where you want to put it.

You can also explore more functions!

Click "export" and then you can get the photo.
